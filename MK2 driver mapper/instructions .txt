Driver Installation and Device Mapping Instructions
Connect the Amplifier to Your PC

Plug the amplifier into your PC using a USB cable.
[+] Ensure the amplifier's power is turned off before connecting.
Power On the Amplifier

[!] Hold down both the CH1 button and the Panel button simultaneously.
While holding these buttons, turn on the amplifier.
Open the Driver Mapper Application

Navigate to and open the file named Driver Mapper on your PC.
Configure Device and Driver

[-] In the Driver Mapper application:
Select the desired device from the drop-down list.
Choose the appropriate driver from the available options.
Initiate the Mapping Process

Click the button at the bottom of the application to begin the mapping process.
